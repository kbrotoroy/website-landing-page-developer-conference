FAQs.
Frequently Ask Questions

  1. Q: Who can attend the conference?

    A: The conference is open to everyone interested in technology, including software developers, students, engineers, startup founders, product managers, designers, and tech enthusiasts. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, you'll find sessions suited to your skill level.

  2. Q: What's included with my conference ticket?

    A: Your ticket includes access to keynote sessions, technical talks, hands-on workshops (depending on your ticket type), networking opportunities, conference materials, refreshments, and digital recordings of selected sessions after the event.

  3. Q: Will the sessions be recorded?

    A: Yes. Most keynote presentations and technical sessions will be recorded. Attendees with eligible ticket plans will receive access to the recordings after the conference through their registered account.

  4. Q: Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

    A: Yes. Tickets can be transferred to another attendee up to 7 days before the event. Simply log in to your account and update the attendee information, or contact our support team for assistance.

  5. Q: What should I bring to the conference?

    A: Please bring your event confirmation email (or digital ticket), a valid photo ID, and a laptop if you plan to participate in hands-on workshops. We also recommend bringing a notebook, charger, and business cards for networking.
